This is a working MVP — not a product.

Pygmalion MVP is a functional prototype used for research, reflection, and pilot practices.

It demonstrates how non-material human value can be realized and flourish without money, coercion, ratings, or gamification.

What the MVP Does

  • Records acts of conscious participation
  • Registers gratitude without creating obligations
  • Works with non-monetary accounting markers (first contour)
  • Shows a non-material footprint of actions — not scores or ranks

There is no accumulation, competition, conversion into value, or status.

Two Formats — Online & Offline

Online MVP (React)

  • Individual reflection flows
  • Acknowledgment of participation and trust
  • Experimental interface for non-material accounting

Offline MVP (HTML / CSS / JS)

  • Group facilitation and dialogue tool
  • Works without internet or accounts
  • Used in education, therapy, and community settings

This MVP Exists to Be Tested — Not Consumed

We invite researchers, developers, and practitioners to explore, discuss, and evolve this prototype together.
